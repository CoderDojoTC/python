# Neural Networks and MNIST

## Summary

Building on the foundations of the previous chapter, this chapter goes deeper
into neural network architectures. You'll add convolutional layers (Conv2D),
MaxPooling, dropout regularization, and a Flatten layer. Then you'll compile,
train, evaluate, and run predictions on your model against the MNIST dataset.
Brief introductions to CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs, autoencoders, and feature extraction
round out the chapter and point toward future learning.
